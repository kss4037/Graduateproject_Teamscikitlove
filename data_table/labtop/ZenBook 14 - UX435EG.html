Test configuration: ZenBook UX435EG, FHD LCD panel, Intel Core I7-1165G7, 1 TB PCIe 3.0 x4 SSD, 16 GB RAM. Test settings: WiFi enabled, the display brightness set to 150 cd/m2, Screenpad off.
